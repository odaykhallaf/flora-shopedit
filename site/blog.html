Research title: Medicinal and therapeutic properties of the Palestinian thyme plant

Palestinian thyme is considered one of the important aromatic and medicinal plants that flourish in the Palestinian territories. Thyme has been used since ancient times in traditional and folk medicine thanks to its unique healing properties. Thyme contains volatile oils rich in phenolic compounds, such as thymol and carvacol, which have antibacterial and antifungal properties. In addition, thyme is a rich source of antioxidants that help protect the body from damage caused by free radicals.
